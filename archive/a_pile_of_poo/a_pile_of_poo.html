There are two parts of this project: an interactive website and a zine, both based on an article written by Aditya Mukerjee, that discuss the racial issue for Unicode. I focused on the features of different mediums to better present the same content under similar visual system. For example, I chose two different way of annotating to fit the medium I was working on. And for the website, both computer and phone display were considered.

* Due to the platform that I used to create the website, it is private. The video documentation of the site is presented instead.
