This poster set is made for Alec Benjamin's 2019 world tour, “Caught In the Middle.” Starting from the collage of pictures that I took, I focus on the word “middle.” Thus, I create the picture in frame composition, as well as placing the title in the middle of the chair. The tone of the poster and the type choice are both echoing with the sentimental music that the artist make.
