This poster set is made for a past exbition titled “Contemporary Art Expression.” Starting from the collage of pictures that I took, I focus on the texture quality of the picture, as well as how the texts are interacting with the figures. Since the picture quality is more experimental, I choose a more “classic” type to balance things out. Moreover, by layering the text underneath the glove, there is a sense of depth within the pieces. In addition, the monospace type sitting in the mosaic grid of the glove further combines figures and type.
