This project involves creating a remake of the album cover for the song "Last Words" by Kenny Beats. Drawing inspiration from aged and dusted old photographs, the design adopts an abstract and faded aesthetic, akin to the hazy nature of our memories. The visual composition aims to evoke a sense of nostalgia and introspection, reflecting the essence of the song's emotional narrative.
