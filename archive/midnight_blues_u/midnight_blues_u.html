I worked with the artist, Harry Kang, on the visual design for his upcoming collective album, “Midnight (Blues) &U.” After discussing with the artist about the ideal style of the album, which is somehow cold and blue, a vector butterfly is illustrated first, then I converted the form in 3D model to add the metal texture. Two versions of the cover were designed to be applied to different parts of the vinyl.
