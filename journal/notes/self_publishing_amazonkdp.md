## Amazon KDP basics
Amazon KDP stands for <b>Amazon Kindle Direct Publishing</b>. It lets you self-publish books online without needing approval from anyone. And you earn <b>monthly royalties</b> when people buy your book.

## what makes a bestseller?
- <b>Niche research:</b> understanding what readers want
- pick the right genre, topic, and the way to speak to your audience
- delivering it to them in a package they can't resist, convincing them they need what you have
- cover: making a promise to your reader that what's inside is worth their time and money
- pricing
- marketing
- how to get reviews that make people want to buy your book
