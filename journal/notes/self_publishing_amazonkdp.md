## Amazon KDP basics
Amazon KDP stands for <b>Amazon Kindle Direct Publishing</b>. It lets you self-publish books online without needing approval from anyone. And you earn <b>monthly royalties</b> when people buy your book.

## what makes a bestseller?
<ol>
    <li><b>Niche research:</b> understanding what readers want</li>
    <ol>
        <li><b>Keywords:</b> people use for book searching</li>
        <li><b>Right trendiness:</b> popular topics bring you traffic but could also mean more difficult to be seen</li>
        <li><b>Refer Bestsellers:</b> look at their topics and how the author talk</li>
        <ol>
            <li>What do their reviews say?</li>
            <li>What do readers like?</li>
            <li>What do they wish was different?</li>
        </ol>
        <li><b>Trend Duration:</b> would it still sell as time passes?</li>
    </ol>
    <li><b>Book title:</b></li>
    <ol>
        <li><b>Drag attention</b> and <b>Trigger curiosity:</b></li>
        <ol>
            <li>Touch the heart</li>
            <li>Funny</li>
            <li>Being unique</li>
            <li>Psychological words: "secret", "discover", "unlock"</li>
        </ol>
        <li><b>Easy to understand:</b> simple -> clear -> easy to remember -> easy to share</li>
        <li><b>Include Keywords:</b> for a better searching exposure</li>
        <li><b>Sounds of words:</b> title that sounds nicer stay with readers longer</li>
        <li><b>Be honest:</b> it should match what your book is about</li>
    </ol>
    <li><b>Cover:</b> making a promise to your reader that what's inside is worth their time and money</li>
    <li><b>Profitable Publishing Strategy:</b></li>
    <ol>
        <li><b>Target audience analysis:</b> how can your book fulfill your readers' needs? Eg. learning, entertainment, etc.</li>
        <li><b>Pricing:</b> refer to similar books</li>
        <li><b>Promotion:</b> raise attention and discussion</li>
        <li><b>Getting Reviews:</b> that act like a real-user organic advertisement</li>
    </ol>
</ol>

## action guide

<ol>
    <li><b>Clarify your goal:</b> additional income? New career? Time frame?</li>
    <li><b>Research your niche:</b> What's the book about? Who's it for? Why will they care?</li>
    <li><b>Plan the book:</b> outline, content for each chapter, drafts</li>
    <li><b>Plan the timeline</b></li>
    <li><b>Finish first draft:</b> "finishing" is the most important thing</li>
    <li><b>Edit and proofread:</b></li>
    <li><b>Settle book title</b></li>
    <li><b>Create book cover</b></li>
    <li><b>Finalize layout and formatting</b></li>
    <li><b>Create a product page on Amazon:</b> sales pitch</li>
    <ol>
        <li><b>Book description</b> from reader's perspective: what would they gain after reading the book? Will they be entertained? Informed? Inspired?</li>
        <li>Use <b>short paragraphs</b> to make the text easy to read.</li>
        <li>Use <b>Keywords</b> to help readers find your book</li>
        <li>Encourage your readers to <b>leave reviews</b>, but never buy reviews</li>
    </ol>
    <li><b>Price</b></li>
    <li><b>Publish</b></li>
</ol>


## launch marketing guide
<ol>
    <li><b>Final reivew everything</b> with fresh eye setting</li>
    <li>Send messgae about your new book to your <b>core reader group</b> (friends, family, anyone who helped you, and followers)</li>
    <li>Set up an <b>email collection system</b>, and write a welcoming email and share your excitement and passion about the book</li>
    <li><b>Social media:</b></li>
    <ol>
        <li>Why you wrote the book</li>
        <li>Personal story about writing the book</li>
        <li>Picture of the book</li>
        <li>Quotes from the book</li>
        <li>Reader reviews</li>
        <li>Giveaways: copies of the book, fun related things</li>
    </ol>
    <li><b>Outreach:</b> make a list of bloggers or podcasters who talk about books like yours, and send them emails and dms.</li>
    <li>Write <b>another email</b> to your list</li>
    <li><b>Check in</b> with the bloggers and podcasters again</li>
    <li>Make a <b>self-recording video</b> talking about your book, people like to see real human connection</li>
    <li>Plan a <b>book launch event</b>, invite everyone</li>
    <li><b>Follow up</b> after your event. Thank people for coming. Ask what they thought. Get feedback.</li>
</ol>
