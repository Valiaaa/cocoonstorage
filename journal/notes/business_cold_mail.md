## productivity tools daily marketing routine
A job posting is a company announcing what they are <b>lacking of</b> in public. Hiring is their second choice, you just need to reach them <b>before</b> the first choice does.

<ol>
<li><span class="list-item-spaced"><b>Job searching:</b> search for <b>6-8 job titles in your niche</b> posted in the <b>last 24 hours</b> on LinkedIn or Indeed, the matching postings are operational pain with an approved budget.</span></li>
<li><span class="list-item-spaced"><b>Send cold mail:</b> for every matching company, find the hiring manager or the head of department, send a 3 line email by 10am same day referencing the exact role they posted.</span>
